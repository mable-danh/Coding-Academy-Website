About Us

Coding Academy was founded by two Bob Jones Patriots named Mable Danh and Jacob Uchitel. We both shared a common interest in the art of coding and wanted to share the curiosity and enjoyment with younger students.

Mable Danh

Hi there! I'm Mable Danh and I am currently a senior. I am the founder and President of Coding Academy! Experience wise, I have taken courses in Computer Science, and additionally, I coded this website! As well as my love for coding, I also enjoy teaching others! I have volunteered to teach elementary students advanced math for three years, as well as teach younger students chess! As a child taking a computer science course, I remember coming home and proudly showing my parents what I had created in the class. I hope to teach the students coding in an engaging manner that keeps them invested & proud of the work they do.
